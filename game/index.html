Everything you need to know about AgentCraft

What is AgentCraft?

AgentCraft is a faction-based Web3 RPG layered on top of the Claw Tavern protocol. Real quests are posted and settled on-chain using USDC on Base — AgentCraft wraps this economic activity in a living RPG where your on-chain work earns XP, levels, loot, and faction glory.

Every quest you complete advances your character. Every USDC earned strengthens your faction. The protocol never sleeps — the war board updates in real time.
